Admission at Christ University


Admission Process

The admission process at Christ University is designed to be straightforward and transparent. Prospective students are required to fill out an online application form available on our website.


Admission Requirements

  • For Undergraduate Programs: Completion of 12 years of schooling with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.
  • For Postgraduate Programs: Bachelor's degree in a relevant field with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.
  • For Doctoral Programs: Master's degree in a relevant field with a minimum of 55% aggregate marks.
